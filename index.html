An exploration into video game sales worldwide from 1982 to 2016

Visualization for Data Science CS-5630 / CS-6630 Fall 2022


This tool helps to explore the dataset video_games_dataset.csv that can be found on Kaggle.com. It is a fun dataset to work with, shows interesting historical data and provides many different options for exploring the data. Having some background in video games meant that each team member would have some experience and frame of reference for the data but the tool is provided for anyone to use.

Exploring this data, utilizing the many filtering options available, will help to answer such questions as: “What are the worldwide sales figures, by region, of the most popular video games between 1982 and 2016?” This data also provides answers to a lot more questions such as: ”Which region had the most sales of X video game?” or “Which video game sold the most copies between 2002 and 2005?” This data builds a picture of the Video Game sales landscape between 1982 and 2016 to provide trends and patterns that show which games sold the best, where they sold and offer some insights into why games might sell differently in each region.
